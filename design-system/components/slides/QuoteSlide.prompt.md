The verified-quote slide (template G) — cream ground, big Anton quotation, sourced byline.

```jsx
<QuoteSlide
  quote="Trois lions sur un maillot, et le foot qui rentre à la maison."
  quoteAccent="à la maison."
  quoteAuthor="Three Lions, l'hymne de 1996"
  caption="LE MYTHE"
/>
```

The quote must be a real, sourced statement — never invented or paraphrased past recognition. `accent` in the source tool is hard-coded to Les oubliés' vert regardless of the post's actual series; only override it if you have a specific reason to.
