# agent-progress-theater ‚Äî Agent Progress Theater

**intent**: Agent work performed as WORKING-STATE theater ‚Äî a short trigger beat hands the frame to the machine, which then visibly _works_: loaders spin, status phrases swap, dots pulse, counters tick ‚Äî before the receipt arrives as a card whose rows cascade in and CHANGE STATE (badges flip to checks, labels strike through, severity pills read out), or as a conversation thread building message-by-message onto a camera push-in payoff. The subject is the machine performing labor over time. It is NOT a typed prompt awaiting output (no prompt/input is ever typed ‚Äî the trigger is a click, a menu choice, or an already-running scan); NOT `cursor-ui-demo` (at most ONE igniting click here, then the cursor exits and the UI performs itself); NOT `grid-card-assemble` (rows there assemble into a static enumeration and hold ‚Äî rows here are alive: they arrive as agent output and then MUTATE, checking off one by one while the viewer watches).

**roles served**

- Key_Feature (from `agent-progress-theater`): when the feature is the agent doing multi-step work (build a plan / scan a repo / fix a vulnerability / handle infra for you) and the proof is status theater ‚Äî a loader lockup with a typed label, status couplets swapping under an `[accent]` spinner, then a checklist/findings card that populates and checks off in front of the viewer.
- Key_Feature (from `message-thread-payoff`): when the agent's work lives inside a conversation or automation thread ‚Äî user/agent bubbles and tool-call/reply cards popping in sequence, the working state carried by pulsing loading dots or rapidly ticking diff counters, resolved by ONE camera push-in tight on the confirmation line (`[reaction pill]`, "Sent using `[@Bot]`", a thank-you bubble).

**duration**: 4.2‚Äì11.6s (short members are a single card-and-check-off or thread beat at ~4‚Äì5s; long members chain trigger ‚Üí interstitial ‚Üí status swaps ‚Üí receipt card at ~9‚Äì12s; thread payoff spans 4.2‚Äì9.1s)

**shot structure** (a warm flat canvas ‚Äî `[off-white / warm beige / near-white bg]`, optional `[faint grid / dot-grid / wavy-line]` texture; white rounded cards with soft drop shadows; ONE `[working accent]` color reserved for the machine (spinner, status words, active step) and one `[done color]` for completion (checks, "Completed"); camera static or ONE slow move ‚Äî motion is overwhelmingly element-level springs, staggers, and state flips. Two folded sub-shapes ‚Äî **(A) checklist/findings theater** and **(B) message-thread payoff**.)

- **Scene 1 (0.0‚Äì~1.5s) ‚Äî the trigger.** Something asks the machine to work, in ONE beat:
  - _Variant ‚Äî option menu (A)_: a centered white pill card poses `[the question]`; it SPRINGS open downward into a rounded menu ‚Äî `[3‚Äì4 option rows]` fade/slide in staggered, each with a number badge. A cursor enters, hover-dances between rows (a pale `[hover fill]` highlight follows it), and CLICKS the chosen row (~press-down spring); the whole menu scales down toward its center and fades out. This is the only cursor appearance in the shot.
  - _Variant ‚Äî modal click (A)_: close-up of a white modal with `[Dismiss]` / `[action button]`; a hand cursor clicks the action (quick press-down spring); the modal fades away. Optionally followed by a serif `[interstitial line]` on the bare canvas ‚Äî words land staggered, hold, fade out word-staggered as the bg swaps.
  - _Variant ‚Äî already working (A)_: a `[Scan in progress]`-style state ‚Äî a thin `[accent]` arc spinner rotating over a heading + body copy + a `[Starting‚Ä¶]` pill (cursor resting on it, motionless); only the spinner moves. The whole scene then rapidly scales up and fades ‚Äî a push-through exit.
  - _Variant ‚Äî workspace push-through (A)_: a rapid camera push-in THROUGH a multi-panel `[workspace: builder / editor / terminal]` ‚Äî panels scale past the viewport edges and clear away to the bare canvas.
  - _Variant ‚Äî thread opener (B)_: a `[user bubble]` spring-pops in ("`[the ask]`"), OR a stats card pops in whose green/red `[diff counters]` rapidly tick and settle ‚Äî the automation's opening receipt.

- **Scene 2 (~1‚Äì4s) ‚Äî the working state (the machine performs).** The frame belongs to the machine; nothing is clickable. Pick 1‚Äì3 working motifs and CHAIN them:
  - A loader lockup: a spinning `[accent asterisk / arc]` beside a `[working label]` typed on rapidly ("`Buildi` ‚Üí `Building plan‚Ä¶`"), a left‚Üíright shimmer sweep passing through the letters; the spinner may momentarily morph asterisk‚Üîdot and back.
  - Status couplets: 2‚Äì3 centered pairs ‚Äî a dark `[action line]` over an `[accent status word]` ("Thinking‚Ä¶", "Noodling‚Ä¶") with its spinner ‚Äî swapping via quick fades/slides at a steady cadence.
  - A `[scan/tool label]` types/expands rightward to its full string, then SHRINKS and DOCKS to the top-left as a fixed corner header (the canvas now belongs to what it produces).
  - A status heading flips tense as rows land beneath it ("Using `[Tool]`" ‚Üí "Used `[Tool]`"), with a gently pulsing "Thinking" and gray meta-lines ("Exploring `[N]` files‚Ä¶") fading in below.
  - _Variant ‚Äî thread machinery (B)_: the `[agent reply]` fades/slides up, then a monospace `[tool_call]` line appears beneath it ‚Äî small icon + `[tool name]` + three pulsing loading dots; OR an instruction bubble scrolls into view (internal window scroll, frame static) followed by a `[brand logo]` pop-in beside a "Sending message‚Ä¶" row. **The pulse dies the instant the result lands** ‚Äî dots vanish as the payload arrives.

- **Scene 3 (~2‚Äì4s) ‚Äî the receipt cascades in (the payoff engine).** The work materializes as a card that BUILDS:
  - _Variant ‚Äî checklist (A)_: a white `[Progress / summary]` pill or card SPRING-pops in with a bounce, then springs open downward (or the summary card glides UP as a taller `[findings]` panel expands beneath it). Rows cascade in one by one ‚Äî slide-up + fade, staggered ‚Äî each with `[number badge / severity pill]` + `[label]` + optional gray `[meta line]`. Then the STATE MUTATION runs: badges flip one by one from numbered outline to a solid `[done color]` circle + white checkmark (slight scale bounce), the checked label simultaneously strikes through and dims; pending items keep partially-drawn arc outlines animating. End the run mid-list ‚Äî some items checked, some still numbered ‚Äî the work is visibly _ongoing_.
  - _Variant ‚Äî thread payload (B)_: the camera pushes in / pans down centering the `[tool_call]` line as a white payload card expands downward from it ‚Äî 2‚Äì4 light monospace `[key: value]` lines fading in. Then the `[resolution message]` expands into place below (inline `[code chips]` and `[link]` coloring), OR a dark `[thread card]` scales up from a status row to DOMINATE the frame while the background darkens, its `[reply]` expanding into place under a "1 reply" divider.

- **Scene 4 (final ~1‚Äì2.5s) ‚Äî resolve.** Two endings:
  - _Variant ‚Äî hold / scroll (A)_: the finished (or mid-mutation) card stack holds static to the end, OR the viewport scrolls down the final card (fast in the last beat) revealing `[a second heading + numbered list]`, ending mid-list. A slow continuous zoom into the card may run underneath (the header drifts off the top of frame).
  - _Variant ‚Äî payoff push-in (B)_: ONE camera push-in + pan-down lands tight on the payoff line ‚Äî "`Sent using [@Bot]`" / the confirmation + `[thank-you bubble]` spring-in ‚Äî then a `[reaction button]` springs into an active pill with bouncy overshoot and a count. The push eases into a gentle near-imperceptible drift and the clip ends on the close-up. No end card.

**rule mapping**

- pill springs open downward into a menu / panel expands beneath a gliding card / payload card expands downward from a tool-call line ‚Üí `anchored-layout-expand` (edge-anchored container growth: height-masked wrapper + inner counter-translate, container drawn at final size); spring flavor from `spring-pop-entrance`
- option rows / findings rows / task rows stagger in (slide-up + fade) ‚Üí `spring-pop-entrance` (staggered-group form, ‚â§500ms cap) or `gsap-effects` (plain fade+translate stagger) ‚Äî NOT `waterfall-entry` (its binary no-fade arrival law contradicts this dialect's soft fade/slide cascade)
- cursor glides to a row and clicks; hand cursor clicks the modal button ‚Üí `cursor-click-ripple` (move + press) + `press-release-spring` (the button's press-down spring)
- pale hover-highlight fill following the cursor between rows ‚Üí `gsap-effects` (a background fill translated row-to-row; no dedicated rule needed)
- menu scale-down fade exit / scene scale-up push-through exit / palette-for-window swap ‚Üí `scale-swap-transition`
- thin arc spinner rotation / spinning asterisk loader ‚Üí `svg-icon-enrichment` (rotating internal SVG parts via `setAttribute('transform','rotate(deg cx cy)')`; timeline-driven, finite)
- asterisk‚Üîdot morph and back ‚Üí `scale-swap-transition` (two elements morphing at the same center)
- typed-on loader label ("Building plan‚Ä¶") / scan label typing to its full string ‚Üí `discrete-text-sequence` (+ `context-sensitive-cursor` for the caret)
- left‚Üíright shimmer sweep through the loader letters ‚Üí `ambient-glow-bloom` (single-pass traveling sheen) or `css-marker-patterns` (highlight sweep) ‚Äî pick sheen for light-on-text, marker for a drawn band
- serif interstitial word-staggered fade in/out; status couplets swapping on a cadence ‚Üí `dynamic-content-sequencing` (phrase windows) + `discrete-text-sequence` (the whole-state swaps); per-word stagger via `gsap-effects`
- pulsing "Thinking" label / three pulsing loading dots (phase-offset) ‚Üí `sine-wave-loop` (finite repeats; kill the tween at the resolve beat ‚Äî see doctrine note)
- status heading tense flip (Using‚ÜíUsed) / gray meta-lines fading in / final-token snaps ‚Üí `discrete-text-sequence`
- label shrinks and docks to the top-left as a fixed corner header ‚Üí `gsap-effects` (plain scale + translate tween; no dedicated rule needed)
- rapid camera push-in through the multi-panel workspace ‚Üí `viewport-change` (the push) + `multi-phase-camera` (phasing) + optional `motion-blur-streak` (velocity blur as panels clear the frame)
- slow continuous zoom into the receipt card (header drifts off top) ‚Üí `multi-phase-camera` (steady-push phase) or `viewport-change`
- summary card / progress pill / chat bubble / brand logo / file chip spring pop-in ‚Üí `spring-pop-entrance`
- summary card glides up as the findings panel expands beneath ‚Üí `gsap-effects` (the glide) + `anchored-layout-expand` (the panel)
- badge flip: numbered outline ‚Üí solid circle + white checkmark with scale bounce ‚Üí `scale-swap-transition` (outline‚Üîsolid swap at same center) + `svg-path-draw` (checkmark draw-in) + `spring-pop-entrance` (the bounce); the pending‚Üíactive‚Üícomplete progression itself ‚Üí `dynamic-content-sequencing` (a snap state machine, per cursor-ui-demo's workflow-approve-press precedent)
- strikethrough + dim on the checked label ‚Üí `css-marker-patterns` (strike-through draw) + `gsap-effects` (opacity dim)
- partially-drawn arc outlines animating on pending items ‚Üí `svg-path-draw` (partial dashoffset, held mid-draw)
- viewport scroll down the final card / internal window scroll under a static frame ‚Üí `gsap-effects` (transform-only content translate inside a masked window) ‚Äî use `viewport-change` only if the FRAME moves
- green/red diff counters rapid tick-and-settle ‚Üí `counting-dynamic-scale` (numeric proxy count-up; suppress the scale-growth component ‚Äî these tick at fixed size)
- dark thread card scales up from a row to dominate the frame ‚Üí `card-morph-anchor` (row ‚Üí full-frame morph + handoff) with the background darkening as a `gsap-effects` overlay fade
- reply message / resolution line expands into place ‚Üí `spring-pop-entrance` (soft overshoot) or `anchored-layout-expand` for a true downward growth
- reaction button springs into an active pill with overshoot + count ‚Üí `spring-pop-entrance` (the pop) + `press-release-spring` (activation flavor) + `counting-dynamic-scale` (the count, if it ticks)
- camera push-in + pan-down centering the tool call / the payoff line ‚Üí `coordinate-target-zoom` (non-centered target: scale + counter-translate) or `viewport-change`
- slight pull-back then gentle end drift ‚Üí `multi-phase-camera` (pull-back phase + continuous micro-drift; keep the drift near-imperceptible)
- static hold on the final stack ‚Üí no rule needed

**camera modifier** (default is a STATIC frame ‚Äî the theater is element-level; at most ONE real move per shot, chosen from):

- Trigger push-through: a rapid push-in through the opening workspace that clears to the bare canvas ‚Üí `viewport-change` + `multi-phase-camera`, optional `motion-blur-streak`.
- Receipt zoom: one slow continuous zoom into the checklist card across the whole mutation run, letting the header drift off the top ‚Üí `multi-phase-camera` (steady push).
- Payoff push-in (sub-shape B's defining move): static through the build, then ONE push-in + pan-down tightening onto the confirmation line, easing to a micro-drift end ‚Üí `coordinate-target-zoom` / `viewport-change` + `multi-phase-camera` (drift).
- Everything else ‚Äî swaps, cascades, check-offs, scrolls ‚Äî happens on a locked frame (any "scroll" is the content translating inside its window, not the camera).

**doctrine note (idle-motion ban)**: the working-state motifs (spinner rotation, pulsing dots, pulsing "Thinking") brush against motion-doctrine's idle-motion ban ‚Äî here they are DIEGETIC: the pulse _performs_ "the machine is working" and is the narrative content of Scene 2, not decorative breathing. Keep every loop finite, timeline-driven, and seek-safe (`sine-wave-loop` finite repeats, `svg-icon-enrichment` rotation), and kill it at the exact frame the state resolves ‚Äî the corpus does this explicitly (the loading dots vanish the instant the payload card expands; the spinner swaps out with the loader lockup).
